## 1. Purpose & Vision

The goal of this project is to create a **single, scrollable, authoritative webpage** that tells the life story of Alex Merced in a clear, credible, and engaging way.

The page should position Alex as:
- A lifelong learner and educator  
- A technologist and developer advocate  
- A writer, speaker, and community builder  
- Someone motivated by empowerment, pluralism, and optimism  

This is not a résumé page.  
It is a **narrative and credibility hub** that links outward to Alex’s current work.

---

## 2. Target Audience

**Primary audiences**
- Developers and data engineers  
- Conference organizers and event curators  
- Podcast hosts and media  
- Readers of Alex’s books and blogs  
- People discovering Alex through talks, podcasts, or social media  

**Secondary audiences**
- Employers and collaborators  
- Journalists and researchers  
- Community members curious about Alex’s background

## 3. Success Metrics

- Time on page greater than 2 minutes  
- Scroll depth exceeding 75 percent  
- Click-throughs to:
  - AlexMerced.com
